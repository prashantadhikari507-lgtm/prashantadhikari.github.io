Society of Actuaries Exams Cleared

Exam P — Probability

Develops a strong foundation in probability and statistics for modeling uncertain events in insurance and finance.

Exam FM — Financial Mathematics

Covers interest theory, time value of money, and valuation of cash flows fundamental to financial decision-making.

Exam FAM — Financial & Actuarial Mathematics

Introduces life-contingent valuation, reserves, and pricing methods used in actuarial modeling and insurance products.

Exam ALTAM — Advanced Long-Term Actuarial Math

Focuses on advanced survival modeling, annuities, pension benefits, and life insurance valuation techniques.

Exam ASTAM — Advanced Short-Term Actuarial Math

Focuses on general insurance modeling, claim frequency/severity, and credibility theory.
